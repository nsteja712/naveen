My Story

Welcome to my corner of the internet! My journey into web development started a year ago, when I built my first website using HTML and CSS. What began as curiosity quickly turned into a passion for creating digital experiences that make a difference.

Over the past 5 months, I've worked with small projects and solidified my foundation, building everything from e-commerce platforms to complex SaaS applications. I specialize in React, Node.js, and modern web technologies, always staying current with the latest trends and best practices.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me writing about my experiences, contributing to open-source projects, or exploring new technologies. I believe in the power of sharing knowledge and helping others grow in their development journey.

What I Do

  • Full-Stack Development: Building scalable web applications using React, Node.js, TypeScript, and modern databases like PostgreSQL and MongoDB.
  • UI/UX Design: Creating intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces with a focus on user experience and accessibility.
  • Technical Writing: Documenting complex technical concepts and sharing knowledge through clear, engaging blog posts and tutorials.
  • Mentoring & Teaching: Helping developers grow their skills and navigate their career path in the tech industry.
